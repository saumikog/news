Issued: 20 December 2025 | District Administration

District Magistrate‚Äôs Official Order Issued On: 20 December 2025 | District Administration In view of the continuously increasing severe cold and dense fog in Agra district, the district administration has taken an important decision keeping in mind the safety and health of school children. Following instructions from District Magistrate Arvind Mallappa Bangari, District Basic Education Officer Jitendra Kumar Gond has issued an order changing the school timings for all schools from classes one to eight. According to the order, from December 20, 2025, until further notice, all schools in Agra district, including government, aided, recognized, and those affiliated with CBSE, ICSE, and other boards, will operate from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. This order has been implemented with immediate effect. The order shall remain effective until further instructions. All school authorities are directed to ensure strict compliance. Any violation of this order will invite action under relevant administrative rules. SOURCE - https://agra.nic.in/
